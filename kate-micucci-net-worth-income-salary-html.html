Janet Micucci (pronounced /mkuti/; born March 31, 1980) is an American actress, comedian, singer-songwriter, and visual artist who has worked in a variety of mediums. After her role as Stephanie Gooch on Scrubs, she went on to play Shelley in Raising Hope and Raj’s girlfriend Lucy on The Big Bang Theory, which gave her her first big television attention. She is a member of the musical comedy team Garfunkel and Oates, with whom she shares a birthday.
