Jimmy Graham (born November 24, 1986) is a tight end with the New Orleans Saints of the National Football League. He is the son of former NFL tight end Jimmy Graham (NFL). After four years of collegiate basketball at the University of Miami, he only played one season of college football for the Hurricanes before being selected by the New Orleans Saints in the third round of the 2010 NFL Draft. Graham recorded 99 catches for 1,310 yards and 11 touchdowns in his second season in the National Football League. It was during that season that he made his first Pro Bowl appearance and was named to the All-Pro team at his position. 

He became the first tight end in Saints history to have more than 1,000 receiving yards in a season when he accomplished this feat. Graham established a Saints franchise mark for catches in a season, and he also matched the club record for touchdowns in a season, both of which were set by Graham (tied with Marques Colston and Joe Horn).
