Joker Jared Leto Photos Shared on Instagram

Publish date: 2024-08-26

Jared Leto is causing a storm on Instagram. The actor-musician has been posting daily photos of himself as the Joker leading up the Monday’s world premiere of Suicide Squad. Leto has been sporadically sharing photos of himself as the clown prince of crime for months, but the latest string began Saturday when the actor posted this photo:
