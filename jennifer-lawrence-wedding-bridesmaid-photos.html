Jennifer Lawrence stars as bridesmaid at brother's wedding

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Jennifer Lawrence has landed yet another magazine cover, this time starring as a beautiful bridesmaid.

The Hunger Games actress was featured in the special issue of Martha Stewart's Real Weddings, as part of the bridal party for her brother Blaine Lawrence's wedding to his now-wife Carson Massler.

Opting for a strapless version of the J. Crew bridesmaid dresses with a sweet heart-shaped neckline, Jennifer played her most ethereal role yet. The 23-year-old stole the show in her cream, floor-length gown and perfectly side-swept pixie hair.

Jennifer on the cover of the spring issue

As she stood on the edge of the group alongside Carson and ten other bridesmaids, Jennifer gave her best winning smile to the camera. The bride, meanwhile, donned a gorgeous white lace Monique Lhuillier gown with floral overlay.

Nicholas Hoult opens up about dating Jennifer

On the magazine cover, Jennifer and the other bridesmaids are pictured all wearing gold matching pendant necklaces — a gift presented to each girl from the bride. In contrast, photos from the inside spread show Jennifer bare necked and wearing dark pearl stud earrings.

The wedding was held in Jennifer and Blaine's home state of Kentucky at Hermitage Farm, and took place last October. The beautiful photos will be released in the spring special issue of the magazine.

Nicholas Hoult and Jennifer first met on the set of X Men in 2011

"We wanted a place that represented Kentucky," Blaine told the American publication, while his nurse wife added, "We kept the day's colours as understated as possible because we wanted the natural beauty of the land to stand out."

The groom, a summer camp director, wore cowboy boots to his wedding on the horse farm. Blaine and Carson tied the knot in front of 325 guests, on one of the rainiest days in Kentucky's recent history.

As for her own plans to walk down the aisle, Jennifer is happily dating child star Nicholas Hoult. The notoriously private couple met and started dating in 2011 on the set of X-Men: First Class. While Jennifer and Nicholas split in January 2013, they rekindled their romance in July of that year.

The About A Boy star recently gushed about his Oscar-winning actress girlfriend, saying that Jennifer "deserved it all".
