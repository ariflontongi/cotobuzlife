Mo Vlogs or Mohamed Beiraghdary is a YouTuber, VLogger who is more popularly known by the alias, Mo Vlogs. He has around 5 million subscribers on his channel. He initially started his YouTube career as a gamer with a channel called HitspecK0 in 2011. He then started vlogging and streaming his daily life on YouTube.

Mohamed is originally from Dubai, but together with his family they moved to London for a while until they finally returned to their home country of Dubai. Let’s get the inside story of this vlogger’s life and more.

Family, life and career of Mo Vlogs

Mo Vlogs o Mohamed was born on March 8, 1995 in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, making him an Emirati. He was born to his parents Nadereh Samimi, his mother and father Esmail Beiraghdary. Mo Vlogs is not the only child in the family. If you have seen his videos and know his, he also has a sister. Very popularly known as the sister of Mo Vlogs, she is called Parisa Beiraghdary but she is known as Lana. Mohamed along with his mother and his sister decided to move to London for Lana’s education. They lived in London for a few years but Mo’s father did not move in with them. Later, when they returned to the Emirates, they began to live separately from their father. The reason for this is unknown.

Mo started a gaming channel inspired by youtubers like Markiplier, under the name HitspecK0 in 2011. The channel wasn’t really a success, it had around 8000 subscribers and 97 videos. Later in the year 2013, he created a new channel “Mo Vlogs” for the purpose of VLogging. His first video there was, “My First Vlog”. Finally, he received a good number of views on his Youtube videos. Later, the Vlogger started uploading some videos covering topics like “Rich Kids from the Middle East: The Most Expensive Car Event in the World!!!” Similarly, there were some other videos like “Racing in my Friends Bugatti!!! ”“ THE LUXURY LIFESTYLE IN DUBAI – BILLIONAIRE BOYS ”and“ Paying CASH for my new car!!! All of which earned him maximum exposure and popularity. The videos also they also got 3 to 10 million views. Likewise, his YouTube channel saw an increase in subscribers.
