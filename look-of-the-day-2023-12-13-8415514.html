Jennifer Lopez's Holiday Look Featured a Low-Key Shirt and Super Extra Heels

Publish date: 2024-08-25

Jennifer Lopez knows how to piece together an outfit. Just look at her plaid peek-a-boo bra ensemble or pantless look as proof. She never disappoints, and she’s always successful in making me want to recreate her looks ASAP. Case in point: that holiday-approved uniform I’m head over heels for. 

Lopez recently took to Instagram to post a carousel of jaw-dropping holiday shots. Step one of her festive attire? A poplin shirt from Dior. If you’ve followed my work, you know I’m all about staple tops like this, as they go with everything, making the timeless piece incredibly easy to style. Lopez seems to understand this; she tucked her designer shirt into an embroidered gold skirt, proving the shirt's inherent versatility. 

Nordstrom Classic Poplin Shirt

Larroudé Miso Ankle-Strap Platform Sandal

Securing your own classic shirt isn’t difficult, either. Madewell makes my all-time favorite version, with an oversized fit, sleek collar, and streamlined buttons. Nordstrom offers a more fitted option, while this Dockers find has more understated buttons, similar to J.Lo’s.

Lopez, being the fashion guru she is, didn’t stop there. Instead, she slipped on a pair of festive shoes. Her tall, glittery Gucci pumps featured gold trimming and a sweet bee decal. Larroudé gives you a similar look, boasting a thick ankle strap and chunky heel. Not to mention, it’s decked out in the same silver sparkles. For a more low-key version, consider these Badgley Mischka glitter sandals. They include the staple elements, but provide a slimmer silhouette.     

Lopez-inspired footwear is perfect for the holiday season, too; wear them to a Christmas Eve dinner and style them to a New Year’s Eve party alike. But don’t get it twisted; they can be dressed up or down. Wear them with an elaborate skirt like J.Lo, or go more casual with wide-leg jeans and an all-black bodysuit. If it’s a bit chilly out, layer on a pair of socks for a chic fashion moment. Best of all, they’re not just good for the winter season. Because of the versatile silver color, they can be worn year round, making them a multi-season must have.   

Get J.Lo’s look by shopping white poplin shirts and sparkly heels from Amazon and Nordstrom, below.
