A Quick Look at the Top 10 Most Commented Posts on Instagram!

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Instagram, the land of celebrities, cute babies, incredible talents, and adorable pets. But who’s winning the race for the most comments? We’ve dived into the Instagram ocean to find the top 10 most commented posts, and you won’t believe the surprises we’ve uncovered.

Alexandros Kopsialis – 43.2 Million Comments – 23.2 Million Comments 

🎁 Greek influencer Alexandros Kopsialis takes the crown with an astounding 43.2 million comments! What’s the catch? It was a giveaway post! The prizes included a PlayStation 5, a Fiat 500, an iPhone 12 Pro, and a lavish La Vie En Rose cosmetics collection.

Bizarrap and Paulolondra

🎵 Argentine DJ Bizarrap challenged fans to leave 23 million comments on his #session23 post featuring Paulo Londra. Fans came through, with 23.2 million comments and 7 million likes!

MrBeast – 21 Million Birthday Giveaway

🎉 MrBeast, known for his generosity, celebrated his birthday with a giveaway. Five lucky followers got a chance to win $10,000 each. Just follow him, share the post, and tag a friend in the comments, and who knows, you could be the lucky one!

XXXTentacion – 10.7 Million Farewell Comments

🌟 The late American rapper XXXTentacion touched hearts even after his passing. His post garnered 10.7 million comments, becoming a tribute to his incredible talent and enduring memory.

Andrea Stramaccioni – 9.6 Million Welcoming Comments

⚽ Italian football manager Andrea Stramaccioni welcomed his fans to his Instagram account with a single post that received over 9 million comments. Surprisingly, this is his only post, but it’s worth checking out!

Gorgeous Armenia – 6.5 Million Reactions

🌄 With only 58.5k followers, Gorgeous Armenia’s first post, a collage of Armenian videos, captured the hearts of Instagram users, amassing an astonishing 6.5 million comments.

Sidhu Moose Wala – 4.75 Million Music Comments

🎶 Indian Punjabi musician Sidhu Moose Wala set a record with 4.7 million comments on his Instagram post. His music resonated with fans, leading to this remarkable response.

The World Record Egg – 3.34 Million Comments

🥚 Remember the famous egg picture that once held the record for the most-liked image on Instagram? Well, it also received a whopping 3.34 million comments!

Alexander Sorloth – 3.32 Million Football Enthusiasm

⚽ Alexander Sorloth, a forward for RB Leipzig, drew 3.32 million comments from passionate Trabzonspor fans eager for his return. It goes to show that football is more than just a game; it’s a feeling.

One Direction’s Campfire – 3.2 Million Family Comments

🔥 One Direction shared an animated image of four lads sitting around a campfire with Family Guy’s Stewie Griffin. The post received 3 million likes and 3.2 million lively comments, sparking rumors of a new family in the making.

Conclusion:

Instagram is a world of surprises, from generous giveaways to heartfelt tributes, and even animated family gatherings. These top 10 most commented posts prove that Instagram is a platform where every post can become a sensation. Likes and comments have the power to turn an ordinary post into an extraordinary one. So, keep scrolling, liking, and commenting – the next viral post might just be a click away
