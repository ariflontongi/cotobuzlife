Billionaire daughter Temi Otedola shares thoughts about stretch marks, calls them 'beautiful'

- Temi Otedola recently joined the body positivity movement on social media

- In a recent Twitter post, the billionaire daughter and fashionista talked about stretchmarks

- Temi questioned why society led people to believe it was not beautiful

One of the things preach in the body positivity movement is the need for self-acceptance and it appears Temi Otedola is on track with this one.

The third daughter of billionaire Femi Otedola recently took to Twitter to opine that stretch marks were beautiful, questioning why society led people to believe otherwise.

In her words:

"Stretch marks are so beautiful. how did we let society tell us otherwise??"
