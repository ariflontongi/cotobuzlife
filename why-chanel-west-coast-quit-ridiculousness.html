Why is Chanel West Coast leaving Ridiculousness?

Publish date: 2024-08-28

FOR OVER 10 years, Ridiculousness has been co-hosted by Rob Dyrdek, Chanel West Coast, and Steelo Brim.

However, one of the MTV personalities decided to part ways with the show.

Why is Chanel West Coast leaving Ridiculousness?

To pursue other endeavors as an executive producer, Chanel West Coast announced she is leaving Ridiculousness.

On March 28, 2023, the TV star revealed to Variety: "After years of being a part of the Ridiculousness family, I’m sad to say goodbye to the show but very excited to announce my next big venture with MTVE/Paramount.

"It’s time for me to step off the red couch and step into the role of executive producer.

"My passion has always been to create, whether it is music, clothing, or film and TV."

She stated: "I plan to bring some of my wildest ideas and dreams to life through amazing film & television projects, build my teams, and work with other creatives paving the way on the screen.

"I’m also very excited for the world to see me beyond ‘the laugh’ and for once, see the real me."

Alongside Rob Dyrdek and Steelo Brim, Chanel has co-hosted Ridiculousness for 30 seasons.

She has been with the series since it first premiered on August 29, 2011.

What is Chanel's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Chanel's fortune is estimated to be $3million.

Before joining Ridiculousness, she and Rod Dyrdek were the hosts of Fantasy Factory.

She was a presenter on both shows until the latter series concluded in 2015.

Outside of television, Chanel is also a rapper and singer.

Her first mixtape Now You Know featured collaborations with French Montana, Snoop Dogg, Robin Thicke, and Ty Dolla $ign.

On October 22, 2020, she released her debut studio album America's Sweetheart.
