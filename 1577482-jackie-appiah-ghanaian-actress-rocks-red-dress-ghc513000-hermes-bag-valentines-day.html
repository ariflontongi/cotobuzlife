Ghanaian actress Jackie Appiah has joined the female celebrities who slayed in red outfits for the 2024 Valentine's Day celebration.

The mother-of-one looked gorgeous in a short-sleeve red ankle-length dress styled with green designer shoes with embellishment.

The beautiful role interpreter wore a long, lustrous, centre-parted frontal hairstyle that reached her bust level as she flaunted her expensive Hermes bag worth GH¢513,000 (N62.6m).

She accessorised her look with a Van Cleef & Arpels bracelet, a pearly bracelet on her left wrist and a gold wristwatch.
