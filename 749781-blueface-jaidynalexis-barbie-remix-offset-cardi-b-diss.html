Blueface Sends Disses Toward Offset, Cardi B, &amp; Soulja Boy On Remix Of Jaidyn Alexis' Hit &quot;Barbie&quot;

Publish date: 2024-08-29

A couple of months ago, Jaidyn Alexis shocked the music world with a decent banger called "Barbie." Her man and MILF Music label founder, Blueface, had a huge hand in the song's success. The Cali native had writing credits and it is a big reason why so many are vibing with it. Jaidyn and Blueface are back for more, with a remix.

However, this is not just a new version with a full verse from the "Thotiana" hitmaker. He is sending shots right at Offset, Cardi B, and Soulja Boy. He raps, "Offset upset Cardi got the update / Soulja Boy mad he found out his b**** f****d late." Of course, the first part of those lyrics is a reference to the time Blueface revealed that the Migos member hooked up with Chrisean Rock.
