The popular rapper helped 50,000 residents of Houston by providing them all with hot meals. The superstar and his Cactus Jack Foundation teamed up with Mayor Sylvester Turner to launch an emergency food programme on Friday, February 26.

The programme is expected to feed 50,000 residents who have been affected by Texas' historic freeze.

In other news, Legit.ng earlier reported that a policeman fed a poor man on the street.

The policeman was on duty when he received a call to assist a caller at a community. The policeman and his team found a man lying motionless.

After they checked the man's pulse, it was realised that he still had life in him.

The policeman asked the dying man what was wrong and he motioned to the policeman that he had not eaten in a long time. Moved by the man's predicament, the policeman rushed to get some fufu for the man.
