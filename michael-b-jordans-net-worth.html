Michael B Jordan's Net Worth 2023

As of 2023, Michael B. Jordan has an estimated net worth of $25 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. Born in Santa Ana, California, and raised in Newark, New Jersey, Jordan began his acting career with minor roles such as Rideland Kid in 1999 and a recurring role on HBO's The Wire in 2002. He then appeared in popular TV shows such as All My Children, Friday Night Lights, and Parenthood.

According to Variety, Jordan earned between $3-4 million for his role in the second Creed film, which grossed $173.6 million in its first two films. He also earned a reported $2 million for his portrayal of Erik Killmonger in Black Panther, which went on to make $1.35 billion worldwide. Jordan recently reprised his role as Killmonger in Black Panther 2: Wakanda Forever in a cameo appearance.

Jordan's ultimate goal is to create intergenerational wealth for his family, as he mentioned in an interview with GQ. He said, "I want to make this thing so my family ain’t gotta worry about nothing. My mom and dad, my brother and sister, my nieces, my future nieces and nephews, my future kids—everybody is going to be good."
