Photos of Ghana-Must-Go Bag Selling for Over N860k in America Stir Massive Reactions

Publish date: 2024-08-18
  • Social media users appear surprised and confused as a picture of one 'Ghana must go' bag selling at $2090 (N860,390.30) surfaced online
  • The price of the bag was confirmed by Legit.ng from the official website of Balenciaga USA
