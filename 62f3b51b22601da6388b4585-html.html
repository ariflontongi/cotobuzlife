The role Amber Heard has been offered in an X-rated movie

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Actress Amber Heard has been offered the chance to star in an X-rated movie, which would result in a very significant pay check.

She can't be very picky about what job offers she takes after losing her much-publicised defamation trial against former partner Johnny Depp, after which she was ordered to pay over 10m dollars.

The negative publicity has also limited her job opportunities right at this time when she needs income more than ever and she has apparently lost a lot of her screen time from the upcoming DC film 'Aquaman and The Lost Kingdom'.

Those high up in the industry have lost trust in her, resulting in a lack of work.

What is Amber Heard's next movie?

However, it appears the actress may have found some silver lining in all this as news has come out that an adult entertainment business has offered her a role to star in a sexy X-rated movie.

The agency, Zen Models, is said to be willing to pay the actress 9m dollars to star in the upcoming project.

The actual contract is reported to be worth 8m dollars and includes an additional 1m dollars donation to the Children's Hospital of Los Angeles.

This would almost cover the debt Amber Heard owes to Johnny Depp, although it wouldn't get her back into featuring in major tent pole films.

Heard's attorney, Elaine Bredehoft, allegedly received a letter from the president of Zen Models, Veronica Madjarian, containing the details of the contract.

"Zen Models is an adult modelling agency and production company," the letter reportedly started.

"We've been in contact with a group of adult film production companies that are interested in offering Ms Heard a contract to perform in an adult entertainment video production."

It remains to be seen if she will take the role or if she will focus on getting more work within Hollywood instead.
