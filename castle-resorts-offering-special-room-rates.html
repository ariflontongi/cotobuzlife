Castle Resorts offering special room rates | News, Sports, Jobs

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Castle Resorts & Hotels, which includes the Kamaole Sands and Polynesian Shores on Maui and Molokai Shores, is offering 35 percent off best available rates in its “New Year, New You” sale.

The offer is valid on trips booked now through Jan. 27 for stays through Nov. 30 with no blackout dates, the hotel company said Tuesday.

Other properties include the Halii Kai at Waikoloa, Hilo Hawaiian Hotel, Kanaloa at Kona, Kona Bali Kai, Kona Reef and Waimea Country Lodge on Hawaii island; Kaha Lani Resort, Kauai Kailani Resort, Kiahuna Plantation & The Beach Bungalows, Makahuena at Poipu, The ISO and Poipu Shores on Kauai; and Waikiki Grand and Waikiki Shore on Oahu.

Restrictions apply. Guests can book by category or by specific unit. The 35 percent off best available rate online discounts for transactions booked during the promotion are nonrefundable and require full prepayment.

The promotion also includes a 25 percent off best available rate option with standard discount and cancellation policies.

For more information or reservations, go to www.castleresorts.com/special-offers/new-years-sale-nonrefundable/ or call (855) 437-6325.
