Toni Braxtons son who is a model, actor and producer after overcoming autism

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Top American singer and songwriter, Toni Braxton, is proud of her son, Diezel Braxton, as he achieved a great milestone signing to Wilhelmina Modeling Agency.

It should be noted that the teenager had a very challenging childhood as he battled autism. His triumph over the health condition inspired many.

In speaking on how her son beat autism, the mother said that early detection of the condition is very important, adding that she is one of the lucky few whose child beat autism.

Another thing worthy of note is that autism is a rare medical condition where patients, among other things, overfocus on a particular interest and have great difficulty in social communication.
