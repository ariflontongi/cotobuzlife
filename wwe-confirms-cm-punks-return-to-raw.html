WWE Confirms CM Punks Return To RAW

Publish date: 2024-08-30

WWE has big show planned for their January 1st episode, as they kick things into high gear. This will include a night of unexpected events at the Day 1 RAW, but now we know one thing to expect for next week.

Ringside News provided comprehensive coverage of WWE RAW’s January 1st’s Day One episode.

WWE advertised CM Punk’s return for RAW next week. He will be a part of the January 8th episode, but he was actually around for RAW this week.

Ringside News previously reported that CM Punk was backstage at the Day 1 RAW. It seems that he showed up, even though he wasn’t booked. This is either a sign that WWE needed him around for another reason, or Punk really likes showing up to work that much that he is making the trip even if he’s not scheduled to appear on television.

It was not noted that CM Punk will do during the January 8th episode of WWE RAW. He is scheduled to compete in the 2024 Men’s Royal Rumble match on January 27th.

Ringside News will be providing live play-by-play coverage of tonight’s show over in our WWE Monday Night RAW coverage hub. It promises to be a memorable episode for many reasons, so it remains to be seen how the episode will turn out.
