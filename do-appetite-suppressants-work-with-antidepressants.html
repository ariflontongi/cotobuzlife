Do appetite suppressants work with antidepressants?

Publish date: 2024-08-28
Appetite suppressants can interact with medications, including antidepressants and anti-anxiety drugs. They also worsen certain health conditions. The medications aren't safe for pregnant or breastfeeding women.

Can you take fat burners while on antidepressants?

They may mix with your medications, especially if you are on antidepressants and anti-anxiety drugs. Fat burner pills lead to hormonal and chemical changes in the body. Therefore, these pills can worsen certain health issues. Pregnant and breastfeeding women should not take fat burner pills.

How can I lose weight while on antidepressants?

Increasing both aerobic exercise and strength training, ideally four times a week for a minimum of 30 to 45 minutes, can increase muscle tone and metabolism and reduce fat stores. Regular exercise has also been shown to reduce risk for depression relapse when combined with a stable medication regimen.
