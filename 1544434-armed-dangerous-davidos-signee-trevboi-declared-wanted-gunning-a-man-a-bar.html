He Is Armed &amp; Dangerous: Davidos Ex-Signee Trevboi Declared Wanted for Gunning Man Down at

Publish date: 2024-08-09
  • A young man named Trevboi has been declared wanted, armed and dangerous after reports about him killing someone at a nightclub in Lagos went viral
  • According to reports, Trevboi used to be an artist signed to Davido's DMW record label but was let go
  • PRO for the Nigerian police Lagos command, Ben Hundeyin, in a tweet, disclosed that a manhunt for Trevboi has commenced, and investigations have began

Internationally renowned Afrobeat superstar, Davido trends online after an ex-signee, Trevboi, was declared wanted online.

A tweep with the handle @cloud_target noted that Trevboi is armed and dangerous while disclosing how he killed his friend at Bar 38, Fadeyi, Lagos.

The tweep, who first publicly published a tweet calling for the arrest of Trevboi, the former Davido signee, is a cultist and used to be a member of the DMW label.

According to reports on the page of the Lagos State Police PRO, Ben Hundeyin, investigations are already ongoing.

Hundeyin noted that Trevboi abandoned his car and fled on foot after escaping the crime scene where he killed a young man at a nightclub in Lagos.

Trevboi has been accused of being a notorious cultist who has terrorised the Bariga area of Lagos for years.
