Zack Orji biography

The famous actor was born on October 10, 1960, in Libreville, Gabon. He grew up in Cameroon, Togo, and the Benin Republic, all in West Africa. Zack is the eldest child in a family of seven children.

The actor enjoyed fetching firewood during his childhood days and was of great help to his mother, who was in the soft drinks business.

His father’s dream for him was to study medicine. However, the actor was absent during the exam day, which resulted in automatic disqualification for that year.

He then relocated to Nigeria after completing his primary and secondary school education. He joined the University of Nigeria Nsukka, where he graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Estate Management.

After completing his tertiary education, he started making shoes. However, his shop was broken into, and several items were stolen.

Career

The talented actor developed an interest and passion for acting at a young age. His debut was in the film known as Unforgiving Sin (1991).

Since then, he has featured in more than 200 movies, which is a huge accomplishment. The actor has also ventured into the real estate business and entrepreneurship.
