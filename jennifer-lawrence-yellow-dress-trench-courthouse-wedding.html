See Jennifer Lawrence Wear a Yellow Dress and Trench to Attend Courthouse Wedding

Publish date: 2024-08-17

Jennifer Lawrence continued her NYC fashion streak while supporting her friend's special day.

Yesterday, the No Hard Feelings actor was spotted while arriving at a downtown NYC courthouse, where she attended the wedding of an unnamed friend. Though she stayed low-key for the event, the Oscar winner took a moment to wave at the photographers who captured her summery ensemble.

Her look was comprised of a classic olive green trench coat, which she layered over a semi-sheer yellow dress with a high neckline, a fitted midi-length bodice, and long sleeves that peeked out from under the jacket. She added nude peep-toe heels and a pair of large square sunglasses from Lu Goldie, while wearing her blonde hair loose in subtle waves.

The sweet appearance came after a busy week for the star, who filmed two commercials filled with stylish looks. The star channeled quiet luxury with her on-duty outfits, which included a layered head-to-toe ensemble from The Row, a luxe, structured minidress made of suit fabric, and a sophisticated black midi-length gown with a fully backless silhouette.

While Lawrence has stayed mostly private about her life with husband Cooke Maroney and their baby son, Cy, the actress recently shared her reaction to her then-boyfriend's proposal during an appearance on Amelia Dimoldenberg's YouTube series Chicken Shop Date, describing the moment as, "Terrifying, but very, very exciting."

"I didn't say what I wanted to. I imagined it a million times, and then I ended up just going, 'What? What? What?' And then going, 'Thank you, thank you, thank you.'"
