About

NFL cornerback and special teams player that was originally drafted by the Carolina Panthers as a third-round pick in 2016. Since then, he has also played for the Philadelphia Eagles, Las Vegas Raiders, Dallas Cowboys, Buffalo Bills, Arizona Cardinals, and the Detroit Lions. During the 2021 and 2022 NFL seasons, he played with the Baltimore Ravens.

Prior to being drafted, he played college football at West Virginia University. Following his 2015 NCAA season, he was named a member of the first-team All-Big 12.

He made his official NFL debut playing against the Denver Broncos during week one of the 2016 regular season. He recorded exactly 64 tackles, 2 interceptions, and 1 sack during his 16-game rookie season.

He was born and raised in the Philadelphia, Pennsylvania area.

He was once packaged in a trade that also included Torrey Smith.
