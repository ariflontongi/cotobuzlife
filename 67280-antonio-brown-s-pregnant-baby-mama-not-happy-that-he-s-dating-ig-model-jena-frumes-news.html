Antonio Browns Pregnant Baby Mama Not Happy That Hes Dating IG Model Jena Frumes

Publish date: 2024-08-17

Pittsburgh Steelers wide receiver Antonio Brown has been spotted out recently and sharing photos on social media with his new girlfriend, Instagram model Jena Frumes- and that has become a big issue with his pregnant baby mama, Chelsie Kyriss.

Kyriss is currently pregnant with her and Antonio's third child and is not at all pleased that AB is posting photos with Jena while she holds down their home.

Brown and Kyriss have reportedly been together for six years and just last month he had shared pictures of her baby bump on Snapchat, but that all came to an abrupt stop once he started dating Jena and posting photos with her instead.

Kyriss recently took to instagram to vent about the disrespect, though she later deleted the post and deactivated her account.
