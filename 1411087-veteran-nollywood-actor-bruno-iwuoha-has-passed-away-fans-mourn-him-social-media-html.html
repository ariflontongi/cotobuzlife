Veteran Nollywood Actor Bruno Iwuoha Has Passed Away, Fans Mourn Him on Social Media

Publish date: 2024-08-14

- Thespian Bruno Ihuoha has passed away after years of battling a health challenge

- Filmmaker Derik Zai broke the news in a social media post

- Condolences messages have poured in for the family members of the deceased

The Nigerian movie industry has lost another rare gem, Bruno Ihuoha, to the cold hands of death.

The respected movie veteran passed away at the National Hospital in Abuja, Okrote’s Blog reports.

It was also reported that the late Ihuoha had been battling with diabetes for years and had also lost his sight to the sickness.

Also confirming the death of the actor, Nollywood filmmaker, Derik Zia, shared a post on his Instagram page mourning the thespian.
