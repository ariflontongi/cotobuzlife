Where to Find the Top Gear Items

Akame’s Hideout – Your One-Stop Shop for Quality Gear

To enter Akame’s hideout, gather enough funds and Points by completing her Requests. Once you meet the requirements, you can buy premium gear directly from Akame. Her inventory includes:

Military Bulletproof Vest – 8,000 Akame Points
Dragon’s Binding – 16,000 Akame Points
Indra’s Amulet – 10,000 Akame Points
Bloody Talisman – 800 Akame Points
Dragon God Amulet – 60,000 Akame Points

Don’t forget about substories; they can boost your Akame Points, so keep an eye out for these side missions.

Mizorogi’s Ad-Hoc Workshop – Another Gateway to Top Gear

Mizorogi’s Ad-Hoc Workshop offers another opportunity to grab high-quality gear. Here’s what Mizorogi has in store:

Iron Breastplate – 431,000 Yen
Eye of the Dragon – 1,138,000 Yen

And for the Payback Ring, you have two choices: find it in Coin Locker C4 within Sotenbori or purchase it at Ebisu Pawn for 22,000 Yen. Despite its lower cost, the Payback Ring is a game-changer for minimizing damage during tough battles.

Exploring the World of Like a Dragon Gaiden

In the narrative of Like a Dragon Gaiden: The Man Who Erased His Name, you step into Kazuma Kiryu’s shoes as he navigates the vibrant Sotenbori district in Osaka and sections of the Isezaki Ijincho district in Yokohama…

FAQs – Your Guide to Common Questions

Q: Where can I find the Dragon God Amulet?
A: You can buy the Dragon God Amulet at Akame’s hideout for 60,000 Akame Points.

Q: How can I earn more Akame Points?
A: Successfully completing substories and side missions will increase your Akame Points.

Q: Where is the Payback Ring located?
A: You can find the Payback Ring in Coin Locker C4 in Sotenbori or purchase it at Ebisu Pawn for 22,000 Yen.
