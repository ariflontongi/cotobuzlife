Lindsay Lohan adds new Italian campaign to her fashion CV

Publish date: 2024-08-07

    No stranger to fashion photography having worked as the face of Miu Miu and Jill Stuart, Lindsay Lohan has ventured further into the world of modelling by appearing in a number of shots for Fornarina.

    The 22-year-old star looks every inch the professional in the photos as she showcases a variety of denim pieces and party dresses from the Italian fashion label's Spring/Summer 09 collection.

    And with her long legs and tousled hair, it is easy to see why the design company chose the New York-born actress to front their new campaign.

    Describing Lindsay as "young, beautiful, eclectic, with great talent and a great personality", the fashion house added that she is "one of the few in the show-biz able to represent the glamorous style of Fornarina."
